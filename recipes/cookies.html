Cookies

Ingredients

  • 125g butter
  • 125g caster sugar
  • 100g light brown soft sugar
  • 225g self-raising flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 200g chocolate chips

Method

  1. Heat oven to 180c.
  2. Cream butter and sugars.
  3. Add egg and vaniilla.
  4. Sift in flour and salt.
  5. Add chocolate chips.
  6. Roll into cookie shapes.
  7. Bake for 8 minutes. Cookies will not look done but remove anyway.
  8. Cool in place until they harden, then transfer for storage.
